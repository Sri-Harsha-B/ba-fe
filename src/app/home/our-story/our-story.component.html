Sri Harsha Baragada

Managing Director

Sri Harsha Baragada (Sri) is the co-founder and CEO of Scremer, a social complaint platform. He also serves as the managing director of Better Analytics Pvt. Ltd. Sri is a passionate advocate for social change and firmly believes that social complaint platforms like Scremer can have a positive impact on society by raising awareness of crucial issues and fostering positive change.

Prior to embarking on his entrepreneurial journey with Better Analytics, Sri worked with clients such as American Express, Charles Schwab, Rabobank during his tenure at Infosys, and also gained experience at KLM Airlines before deciding to leave his job.
